Cranberry-Orange Tea Ring

Essa adoravel tea ring combina os sabores classicos de cranberry e laranja para criar uma que todos irao amar!

Igredientes

  • 1/4 cup warm water (110 degrees F (43 graus C))
  • 1(.25 ounce) package rapid-rise yeast
  • 3/4 cup warm milk (115 degrees F (46 graus C))
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup orange marmalade
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1/2 cup old-fashioned oats
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Filling

  • 1 1/2 cups chopped fresh cranberries
  • 6 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 6 tablespoons water
  • 6 tablespoons chopped walnuts
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon butter
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 egg white
  • 1 teaspoon water

Glaze

  • 1/2 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon frozen orange juice concentrate
  • 1 teaspoon water, or as needed

Passos/modo de preparo

  1. Passo 1: Combine warm water and yeast in a large bowl;stir to dissolve. Add war milk,egg,marmalade, and butter.Stir to continue
  2. Passo: Mix flour, oats, and salt together in a separate bowl.Add yeast mixture and stir until moistened. Knead dough until smooth and elastic, 6 to 8 minutes. Place dough in a greased bowl, turning to coat both sides. Cover and let rise until doubled, about 30 minutes
  3. Passo: Meanwhile, combine cranberries, brown sugar, and water in a small saucepan. Cook over medium heat until cranberries begin to soften, 10 to 15 minutes. Remove from heat;stir in walnuts, butter, and lemon juice. Let cool.
  4. Passo: Punch dough down. Roll dough into a 10x20-inch rectangle on a floured surface.
  5. Passo: Whisk egg white and water together. Brush lightly over the dough. Spread cranberry filling over the dough, leaving a 1-inchhhh border on all sides. Roll up tightly, starting from a long edge. Seal edges and cut off any tapered sides with no filling.
  6. Passo: Transfer roll to a large square baking sheet. Join ends together to form a ring. Use sharp kitcher shears to make cuts at 1-inch intervals, cutting 2/3 of the way through the dough. Twist each slice gently to expose the swirled filling. Gently shape dough into a perfect ring. Cover and let rise until doubled, 20 to 25 minutes.
  7. Passo: Prehead the over to 350 degrees F (175 graus C).
  8. Passo: Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 25 minutes. Cool on the baking sheet for 20 minutes before removing to a wire rack. Let cool completely.
  9. Passo: Stir powdered sugar and orange juice concentrate together in a small bowl. Add enough water to reach a glaze-like consistency. Drizzle glaze over tea ring. Allow glaze to set before slicing

Tempo

  • Prep: 30 mins
  • Cook: 35 mins
  • Additional: 1 hr 10 mins
  • Total: 2 hrs 15 mins
  • Yield: 1 tea ring

Quick Cinnamon Rolls Recipes

Quick and easy cinnamon rolls from scratch, with no yeast, proofing, or kneading necessary!

Ingredientes

  • 1/4 cup butter, divided(Optional)

Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoon butter, softened (Optional)
  • 3/4 cup milk
  • 1 egg

Filling:

  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon

Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 1 cup confectioner's sugar (Optional)
  • 4 ounces cream cheese, softened (Optional)
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened (Optional)
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract (Optional)

Directions

  1. Passo: preheat oven to 400 degrees F (200 graus C). Brush a 9-inch square baking dish with 2 tablespoon melted butter
  2. Passo: whisk flour, 2 tablespoons white sugar, baking powder, and salt together in a large bowl. Work 3 tablespon softened butter into flour mixture using your hands. Beat milk and egg together in another bowl;pour into flour-butter mixture and stir with a rubber spatula until a soft dough forms.
  3. Passo: turn dough out onto a floured work surface and roll dough into a 1/4-inch thick rectangle. Brush surface of dough with 2 tablespoons melted butter.
  4. Passo: whisk 1/2 cup white sugar, brown sugar, and cinnamon together in a small bowl. Sprinkle 1/2 of the cinnamon sugar mixture in the bottom of the prepared baking dish. Sprinkle remaining cinnamon sugar over butter brushed dough. Roll dough around filling to form a log; cut log into 18 rolls and place rolls in the prepared baking dish
  5. Passo: bake in the preheated oven until rolls are set, 20 to 25 minutes.
  6. Passo: beat confectioner's sugar, cream cheese, 1/4 cup softened butter, and vanilla extract together in a bowl until frosting is smooth. Top hot cinnamon rolls with cream cheese frosting.

Tempo

  • Prep: 20 mins
  • Cook: 20 mins
  • Total: 40 mins
  • Servings: 18
  • Yield: 18 cinnamon rolls
